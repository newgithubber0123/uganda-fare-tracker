About Us

At Uganda Transport Fare Tracker, we make your journeys clearer and fairer. Moving around Uganda can be stressful when fares are uncertain or change without notice. That’s why we built this platform — to give you accurate, up-to-date transport fare information for taxis, bodas, and buses across the country. Our goal is simple: help you travel smarter. Whether you’re a daily commuter, a student, or a traveler, we put the power of knowledge in your hands so you can plan your trips with confidence and avoid being overcharged. With Uganda Transport Fare Tracker, you’ll always know what to expect before you hit the road.
